About Islamic Online University Borama

Who We Are

The International Online University (IOU), formerly known as the Islamic Online University, was founded by Dr. Bilal Philips in 2007. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education to students worldwide, aiming to change the Muslim nation and the world through appropriate, Islamized education.

We offer a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, including Islamic Studies, Education, and more, all designed to cater to the diverse needs of our global student body.
